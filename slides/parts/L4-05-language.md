## 跨文化语用校准三步法

<div class="mt-5 grid grid-cols-3 gap-3">

<div class="card" style="border-color:#B8860B;">
  <div style="font-size:1.5rem;font-weight:700;color:#B8860B;text-align:center;">①</div>
  <div style="font-weight:700;margin-top:.3rem;text-align:center;">语义等效</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.78rem;color:#78716C;margin-top:.5rem;text-align:center;">
    不是逐字翻译<br>是等效传递信息
  </div>
  <div class="mt-2" style="font-size:0.75rem;text-align:center;color:#1C2B4B;">
    "敦煌壁画是中华文化瑰宝"<br>
    ↓<br>
    "These murals reveal how Tang Dynasty artists saw the divine"
  </div>
</div>

<div class="card" style="border-color:#B8860B;">
  <div style="font-size:1.5rem;font-weight:700;color:#B8860B;text-align:center;">②</div>
  <div style="font-weight:700;margin-top:.3rem;text-align:center;">文化语境替换</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.78rem;color:#78716C;margin-top:.5rem;text-align:center;">
    比喻/典故/成语<br>替换成英文受众熟悉的表达
  </div>
  <div class="mt-2" style="font-size:0.75rem;text-align:center;color:#1C2B4B;">
    "丝绸之路"<br>
    ↓<br>
    "the ancient trade route that connected China to Rome"
  </div>
</div>

<div class="card" style="border-color:#B8860B;">
  <div style="font-size:1.5rem;font-weight:700;color:#B8860B;text-align:center;">③</div>
  <div style="font-weight:700;margin-top:.3rem;text-align:center;">CTA本地化</div>
  <div style="font-size:0.78rem;color:#78716C;margin-top:.5rem;text-align:center;">
    行动召唤<br>情感共鸣 + 具体行动
  </div>
  <div class="mt-2" style="font-size:0.75rem;text-align:center;color:#1C2B4B;">
    "欢迎购买"<br>
    ↓<br>
    "Reserve your piece of living history"
  </div>
</div>

<div class="mt-4 card" style="font-size:0.72rem;font-family:monospace;background:rgba(30,30,46,.95);color:#E2E8F0;padding:.8rem 1.2rem;line-height:1.8;">
<div style="color:#B8860B;">你是一位专业的跨文化传播文案专家</div>，擅长将中文内容翻译成面向英语受众的地道英文。请遵循以下原则：<br><br>
1. <strong>不直译，要意译</strong>：翻译的不是字词，是信息和情感<br>
2. <strong>补充文化背景</strong>：假设读者对中国文化完全陌生<br>
3. <strong>使用英语母语者的表达习惯</strong>：避免中式英语结构<br>
4. <strong>简短有力</strong>：社交媒体文案每条不超过15个英文单词<br>
5. <strong>结尾要有情感共鸣或行动召唤</strong><br><br>
